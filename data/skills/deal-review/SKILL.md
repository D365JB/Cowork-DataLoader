# Deal Review Brief

Generate a comprehensive deal review brief for a specific customer account.

## Steps

1. **Identify the account**: Ask which customer account to review if not specified.

2. **Gather account intelligence**:
   - Search emails for all recent correspondence mentioning the customer
   - Find any meeting notes, briefing docs, or presentations related to the account
   - Check Teams messages for internal discussions about the deal
   - Look for any shared documents in OneDrive or SharePoint about this customer
   - Check calendar for upcoming meetings with this customer

3. **Build the brief** with these sections:
   - **Account Overview**: Company name, key contacts, deal value, current stage
   - **Relationship Timeline**: Key interactions and milestones to date
   - **Deal Status**: Current stage, next steps, expected close date
   - **Key Stakeholders**: Decision makers, influencers, champions (both internal and customer-side)
   - **Competitive Landscape**: Who else is the customer evaluating? Our advantages and risks.
   - **Recent Communications**: Summary of last 3-5 email threads and meetings
   - **Open Action Items**: What needs to happen next, and who owns each item
   - **Risks & Concerns**: Anything that could derail the deal
   - **Recommended Next Steps**: Top 3 actions to advance the deal

4. **Format as a polished Word document** with:
   - Professional headers and formatting
   - Key metrics in a summary table at the top
   - Color-coded risk indicators (green/yellow/red)
   - Clear ownership for each action item

## Output Format

A Word document titled "Deal Review - [Customer Name] - [Date].docx" ready for pipeline review meetings.
