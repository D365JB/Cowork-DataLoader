# Weekly Executive Status Report

Generate a comprehensive weekly status report for executive leadership.

## Steps

1. **Gather recent activity** (past 7 days):
   - Search my sent and received emails for key updates, decisions, and action items
   - Review my calendar events from the past week — note meetings attended, key outcomes
   - Check Teams messages for important discussions and decisions
   - Look for any documents I worked on or received this week

2. **Organize by category**:
   - **Key Deals & Pipeline**: Summarize active deal progress, new opportunities, and any at-risk accounts. Include pipeline values where available.
   - **Product & Launch Updates**: Any product milestones, launch progress, or engineering updates.
   - **Customer Interactions**: Meetings with customers, follow-ups sent, proposals delivered.
   - **Internal Decisions**: Key decisions made, approvals given, escalations handled.
   - **Upcoming Next Week**: Important meetings, deadlines, and deliverables for the week ahead.

3. **Create the report**:
   - Format as a Word document with clear section headers
   - Lead with a 3-sentence executive summary
   - Use bullet points for readability
   - Flag any items that need leadership attention with "[ACTION NEEDED]"
   - Include a "Risks & Blockers" section if any exist
   - End with "Next Week's Priorities" (top 3-5 items)

## Output Format

A Word document titled "Weekly Status Report - [Date Range].docx" with professional formatting suitable for sharing with the VP team and board.
