COMPETITOR 1: FABRIKAM X200
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Overview:
- Fabrikam's flagship manufacturing automation platform
- Strong brand recognition in European markets
- Recently expanded to North America (2024)

WHERE WE WIN:
✓ Deployment Speed: 8 weeks vs 6 months (our #1 differentiator)
✓ AI/ML Included: Built-in vs $150K add-on (their "AI Suite" is bolted on)
✓ Azure Native: Our IoT integration is seamless; they only support AWS
✓ Modular: Customers can start with 1 line and expand; X200 is all-or-nothing
✓ Total Cost: $800K starting vs $950K + $150K AI = $1.1M for comparable features

WHERE THEY WIN:
✗ European presence - stronger reference base in EU manufacturing
✗ Longer track record (platform launched 2019 vs our 2026 GA)
✗ SAP integration slightly deeper (but we're closing the gap)

LANDMINES TO SET:
→ "How long did your Fabrikam deployment actually take vs what they promised?"
→ "Is AI included in their base price, or is it an expensive add-on?"
→ "Can you start with one production line, or do you have to buy the whole platform?"

OBJECTION HANDLING:
"Fabrikam has been around longer"
→ "That's true for their legacy platform. But X200 is new (2023) - we've been 
   building on modern architecture from day one. And our ProLine S has 3 years 
   of production data showing 18%+ throughput gains."

"Fabrikam is cheaper"
→ "Their base price looks lower, but AI is a $150K add-on and deployment takes 
   6 months vs our 8 weeks. Factor in delayed time-to-value and the total cost 
   of ownership is 30% higher."

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
COMPETITOR 2: NORTHWIND NX-PRO
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Overview:
- Northwind's industrial automation suite
- Strong in legacy/brownfield environments
- Good mid-market presence, especially food & beverage

WHERE WE WIN:
✓ AI/ML: We have native AI; they have zero AI capabilities
✓ IoT: Azure-native vs custom integration (expensive, fragile)
✓ Modern Architecture: Cloud-first vs on-premise with cloud "bridge"
✓ Predictive Maintenance: Built-in vs non-existent
✓ Speed: 8-week deployment vs 4 months

WHERE THEY WIN:
✗ Price: $700K starting (but no AI, no IoT, manual everything)
✗ Legacy compatibility: Better at connecting to 20+ year old PLCs
✗ Food & Beverage vertical: Deeper industry-specific features

LANDMINES TO SET:
→ "Does NX-Pro include any AI or predictive maintenance capabilities?"
→ "What does their IoT integration look like? Is it Azure-native?"
→ "How do they handle predictive maintenance vs reactive?"

OBJECTION HANDLING:
"Northwind is cheaper"
→ "At $700K vs $800K, you're saving $100K but losing AI, IoT, and predictive 
   maintenance. Our customers see $2M+ in annual savings from AI-driven 
   optimization alone. The ROI math is clear."

"We have old equipment that Northwind supports better"
→ "We support all major PLC protocols through our Azure IoT adapter layer. 
   And unlike Northwind, you get predictive maintenance that prevents 
   the costly breakdowns your old equipment is prone to."
